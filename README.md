# FUCK YOU Dimitra: Audio-driven diffusion model for expressive talking head generation - Supplementary Materials

This repository contains supplementary materials for Dimitra: an audio-driven diffusion model for expressive talking head generation.

## Video Examples

### 1. Videos Associated with Main Paper Figures

#### Figure 1 - VoxCeleb Dataset (Figure 3, Left)
![Video](static/videos_Dimitra/output_audio_vox_2_overlay.mp4)
*Example videos on the VoxCeleb dataset corresponding to Figure 3 (left) in the main paper. Dimitra generates the most natural video.*

#### Figure 2 - VoxCeleb Dataset (Figure 3, Right)
![Video](static/videos_Dimitra/output_audio_vox_1_overlay.mp4)
*Example videos on the VoxCeleb dataset corresponding to Figure 3 (right) in the main paper. Dimitra demonstrates the best lip synchronization.*

#### Figure 3 - HDTF Dataset (Figure 4, Left)
![Video](static/videos_Dimitra/output_audio_HDTF_1_overlay.mp4)
*Example videos on the HDTF dataset corresponding to Figure 4 (left) in the main paper. Dimitra generates facial expressions and lip motion corresponding to the stress in the audio.*

#### Figure 4 - HDTF Dataset (Figure 4, Right)
![Video](static/videos_Dimitra/output_audio_HDTF_2_overlay.mp4)
*Example videos on the HDTF dataset corresponding to Figure 4 (right) in the main paper. Sadtalker is unable to close the mouth.*

### 2. Videos Associated with Appendix Figures

#### Figure 5 - Vox Dataset (Ablation, Figure 6)
![Video](static/videos_Dimitra/output_audio_abla_1_overlay.mp4)
*Example videos on the Vox dataset corresponding to Figure 6 in the appendix. Combining Wav2Vec features, phonemes, and text transcript yields the best results.*

#### Figure 6 - "Style Sequences" (Figure 7)
![Video](static/videos_Dimitra/output_style_seq_audio_overlay.mp4)
*Example videos corresponding to Figure 7 in the appendix. Using "style sequences" to encode facial expression can result in identity changes, particularly around the eyes.*
