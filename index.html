Abstract

Dimitra is an audio-driven diffusion model for expressive talking head generation. This model allows for generating high-quality facial animations driven by audio inputs, focusing on lip synchronization and natural facial expressions. Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on multiple datasets, including VoxCeleb and HDTF. This page provides supplementary video examples demonstrating the performance of Dimitra on different datasets and configurations.
